About Me:

Hello! I am Daniel Boghossian, a skillful and industrious college student working on a degree in computer science/game development. Ever since I was a child playing on my old Nintendo DS I knew that I wanted to create video games for a living. My goal is to be able to provide children with the same joy and comfort I felt when I first started playing video games.

My heart is set on Game development, however my strong suit lies in programming. I learned my first coding language in 8th grade, sparking my aspiration to create the mechanics and software elements required to give the games life. Being able to create something with my own hands, especially something that runs on technology and inculcates the advancement of the human race is an art form that I wish to devote the rest of my life too.

Since that first coding class I have learned half a dozen coding languages and created half a dozen games. Everyday I strive to learn something new and to advance my skills and broaden my horizons. I will make an impact on the world.
