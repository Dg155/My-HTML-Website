Cucumber Castle:

  • Creator, Developer, Programmer
  • July 2022
  • (Unreal 5, Blueprints)
  • Duration: 1 week
  • Team Size: 1

Here is my first dip into the vast ocean that is the Unreal Engine. Taking advantage of the new Unreal 5 megascan library, and real time lighting system, I created a beautiful 3D medieval forest terrain to explore. I coded collectable cucumbers scattered throughout the map that increase a total score and motivate exploration!

DaBoys:

  • Creator, Developer, Programmer
  • June 2020
  • (Unity, C#)
  • Duration: 10 weeks
  • Team Size: 3

DaBoys is a 2D Tower Defense game. It was my COVID pastime, and my proudest work. After completing an online course on Unity and C# I created this entire game from scratch, including story, mechanics, and extra game modes. I enlisted help from two of my friends for music and sprites, and in turn decided to make the game about my 4 closest friends and I!
